About Us

PT FindYourTrip Global is a trusted tour and travel company based in Batam, Kepulauan Riau. We specialize in providing authentic, comfortable, and memorable travel experiences for both domestic and international travelers.

Vision

To become a trusted and leading tour and travel company that connects travelers around the world with authentic, comfortable, and memorable travel experiences.

Mission

  • To provide the best travel services
  • To promote Indonesian tourism destinations to the international market
  • To establish international partnerships
  • To support sustainable tourism

Meaning of the Logo

The globe represents the world and global destinations — symbolizing that FindYourTrip is ready to help people explore the world without limits. The airplane circling the globe illustrates journeys that connect the entire world, representing a dynamic, fast, and wide-reaching travel service. The circular motion also signifies an endless journey (continuous journey) and comprehensive service from the beginning to the end of each trip.

Meaning of the Colors

Blue: Represents trust, professionalism, and calmness. Blue is also often associated with the sky and the sea — the two main elements of travel and tourism.

Yellow: This color symbolizes energy, optimism, and happiness — reflecting the positive spirit of traveling and the cheerful service provided.
