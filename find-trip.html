Bali Explorer Adventure

Discover the beauty of Bali, from the cultural heart of Ubud, the stunning landscapes of Nusa Penida, to the iconic sunset views of Kuta, combined with authentic Balinese cuisine.

  • Local transportation & airport transfers

Excluded

Highlights

  • Ubud Rice Terrace

  • Shopping & Culinary Experience in Kuta
Brief Itinerary

Day 1: ARRIVAL DENPASAR – UBUD (Dinner)
Meet at I Gusti Ngurah Rai International Airport and transfer to Ubud. Hotel check-in and short rest. Afternoon orientation tour visiting Ubud Palace, Saraswati Temple, and Ubud Art Market. Dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 2: UBUD – TEGALALANG – TIRTA EMPUL (Breakfast, Dinner)
Breakfast at hotel. Visit Tegalalang Rice Terrace, Tirta Empul Temple, Tegenungan Waterfall, and a local coffee plantation. Return to hotel and dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 3: UBUD – NUSA PENIDA – KUTA (Breakfast, Lunch)
Morning transfer to Sanur Harbor and cross to Nusa Penida. Visit Kelingking Beach, Broken Beach, and Angel’s Billabong. Lunch followed by snorkeling activity. Return to Bali and transfer to Kuta.

Day 4: KUTA – TANAH LOT – JIMBARAN (Breakfast, Dinner)
Breakfast at hotel. Free time at Kuta Beach. Afternoon visit to Tanah Lot Temple for sunset view. Romantic seafood dinner at Jimbaran Beach.

Day 5: KUTA – DENPASAR (Breakfast)
Breakfast and free time until check-out. Transfer to airport for departure. Tour ends.
